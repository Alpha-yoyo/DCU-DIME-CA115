About

Our Think Fitness app engages in all things health and fitness. It has all the metrics required to track your fitness progress gathered in one place. The app can be used more accurately when connected to a fitness tracking device eg: Fitbit or Apple watch but it is not a requirement to have one as the app has plenty of features that do not require one.

It lets you track and calculate how many calories you've burned each day. After entering your details eg: your height and weight, you are provided with your recommended calorie intake and calculates your BMI. There is a barcode scanner installed on the app to allow the user to accurately log information about the food you eat.

It can act as a pedometric counter to ensure you're hitting your 10,000 steps a day and will send you notifications of encouragement! If connected to a fitness tracking device, it can calculate your heart rate.

One of our most original features is the weather detector. The app allows you to enter the route you'll be exercising that day and can inform you of the weather conditions for that day.

Features

  • Displays your calories burned each day
  • Gives you recommended calorie intake and output based on user data
  • Calculates your BMI based on your height and weight
  • Activity data collected by fitness tracker if in use or by smartphone accelerometer
  • Scan barcodes (UPC) and log nutrition information about what you eat
  • Steps counter
  • Gives live heart rate if connected to a fitness tracker
  • Integrated weather tracking and suggestions based on workout patterns
